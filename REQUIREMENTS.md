# Coffee Personality Quiz - Requirements

## Personality → Coffee Pairings

1. **Bold Adventurer** → Double Espresso - "You live for intensity"
2. **Sweet Enthusiast** → Caramel Latte - "Life's too short for bitter"
3. **Health Nut** → Oat Milk Americano - "Wellness in every sip"
4. **Zen Minimalist** → Black Coffee, Single Origin - "Simple. Clean. Perfect."

## Result Display

**Show all percentages** - Primary result highlighted, but user sees full breakdown (e.g., "You're 50% Bold Adventurer, 30% Zen Minimalist, 20% Sweet Enthusiast") with all coffee recommendations.

## Visual Style

- **Base:** Warm & Cozy - earth tones, soft creams and browns, coffeehouse feel
- **Font:** Serif header (Lora), clean sans-serif body (Source Sans)
- **Header:** "Coffee Personality Quiz" in a large, prominent font size
- **No emojis** on answer options - keep text clean
- **No icons** - the background images provide the visual storytelling

## Interactive Background

- Default background: warm, neutral coffeehouse tones (matching Style 4)
- **On hover:** When a user hovers over an answer option, the entire page background changes to a full-bleed image that corresponds to that answer's energy
- Images fade in smoothly on hover, fade back to default when cursor leaves
- Each answer option has its own unique background image

## Quiz Questions

### Q1: You find a mysterious door in your house you've never noticed. What do you do?
- Kick it open immediately → Bold Adventurer
- Make a snack first, then investigate → Sweet Enthusiast
- Check if there's a draft - good airflow matters → Health Nut
- Sit quietly and listen for what's on the other side → Zen Minimalist

### Q2: You're given a blank canvas and told to paint anything. What appears?
- An erupting volcano → Bold Adventurer
- A candy shop from a dream you had → Sweet Enthusiast
- A mountain trail at sunrise → Health Nut
- A single perfect circle → Zen Minimalist

### Q3: An alien lands and asks you to explain humanity in one sentence. You say:
- "We jumped out of planes for fun before we could cure the common cold" → Bold Adventurer
- "We invented dessert before dinner was even figured out" → Sweet Enthusiast
- "We run toward mountains just to say we reached the top" → Health Nut
- "We spend our whole lives learning to be still" → Zen Minimalist

### Q4: You wake up with a superpower. What is it?
- A healing factor that recovers from any wound → Bold Adventurer
- You can conjure any dessert out of thin air → Sweet Enthusiast
- You never need sleep and always feel rested → Health Nut
- You can stop time whenever you want → Zen Minimalist

### Q5: Pick a hypothetical pet:
- A falcon that follows your commands → Bold Adventurer
- A golden retriever that bakes somehow → Sweet Enthusiast
- A dolphin that takes you on morning swims → Health Nut
- A cat that cuddles you in silence → Zen Minimalist
