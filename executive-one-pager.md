# Coffee Personality Quiz — Executive One-Pager

## The Problem

Basecamp Rewards is losing money. 12,400 members signed up, but only 2,100 use it each month. Retention has dropped from 45% to 27%. Days between visits have nearly tripled. Program ROI is below 1.0.

Customers describe the program as "fine but forgettable." Brand NPS is 67. Program NPS is 12. People love Basecamp — they just don't care about Basecamp Rewards.

## The Fix

A Coffee Personality Quiz that gives members a coffee identity (like "Bold Pioneer" or "Cozy Campfire") with personalized drink recommendations. Instead of "you have 43 points," the program says "you're a Bold Pioneer — here's what to try next."

This isn't a guess. The concept surfaced independently across manager notes, campaign drafts, barista observations, and customer feedback. Our baristas already hear "What should I try?" multiple times every shift. No competitor in our market does this.

**Live prototype:** https://quiz-project-psi-seven.vercel.app

## How We'll Know It's Working

| Green Light (proceed to rollout) | Red Light (kill it) |
|---|---|
| 30-day retention among quiz-takers ≥ 38% (up from 27%) | Retention shows no improvement over non-takers |
| Quiz completion rate ≥ 50% | Completion rate below 35% |
| Barista feedback is net positive | Baristas say it hurts the floor experience |

## The Math

Spending $7,810 to test whether we can save a $40K investment — or walk away with clear data. Full rollout if approved: $75-83K total. Doing nothing means the program continues losing money until leadership kills it.
